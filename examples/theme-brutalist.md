## DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The brutalist theme delivers:

- **RAW POWER** - High contrast black and white
- **BOLD TYPOGRAPHY** - Uppercase, heavy weights
- **GEOMETRIC FORMS** - Sharp edges, thick borders
- **NO DECORATION** - Function over form
