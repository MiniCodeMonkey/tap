# Minimal Theme

Clean, Apple-inspired aesthetics

---

## Design Philosophy

The minimal theme embodies:

- **Simplicity** - Clean white backgrounds
- **Clarity** - High contrast text
- **Focus** - Content takes center stage
- **Elegance** - Subtle transitions and spacing
